Booking Deposit Payment

  1. Nepal Trips

    For Nepal Trips, you will have to make a non-refundable deposit of 25% of trip cost at the time of booking the trip. You can pay the remaining amount upon your arrival in Kathmandu and before the trip departure.

    The non-refundable deposit should be sent to the Company or its Agent. The Company’s Tailor Made trips may require a higher deposit or full payment at the time of booking. If deposit is different from that outlined in this clause, you will be advised by the company at the time of booking.

  2. India/Tibet/ Bhutan Trips:

    At time of booking a non-refundable deposit of 25% is due along with due airfares (if client wants the Company’sy to arrange it) before 21 days of trip departure. If you're booking 21 days or less prior to departure,full payment is due.
